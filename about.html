Learn More About Stephy.

Stephany Blair is a graduate of Pratt Institute with a BFA in interior design. She has been designing apartments for staging and personal clients since 2007.

Steph has her own studio in New York City and is an interior designer who has been involved in the industry for over 12 years. She also provides a wide range of services which are available to both commercial and residential clients. Her design style is said to be eclectic, with a focus on abstraction, color sense, and balance.

She specializes in high-end projects for clients in the arts. Blair has been featured in many publications such as House Beautiful Magazine, Elle Decor Magazine, The New York Times and more.

Steph is a very talented interior designer and is respected for her extensive knowledge of the high-end market. She has gathered this knowledge through years of experience and continues to learn more about design and construction techniques every day.

In her spare time, Steph has taken up more project that include textile design and occasionally modern fashion composition and illustration.
